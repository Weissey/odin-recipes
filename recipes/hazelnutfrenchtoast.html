Hazelnut French Toast

Description

One of the best alternative versions of a traditional french toast, the hazelnut french toast brings a rich distinguished flavor.

If you are allergic to nuts, please feel free to choose another one of my recipes or to just leave out the hazelnuts.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup beaten eggs
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • 2 tablespoons hazelnut liquer
  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided, or more as needed
  • 12 pieces brioche
  • 1 cup toasted hazelnuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup, or to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, half-and-half, and hazelnut liqueur together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Melt 1 teaspoon butter in a large skillet, tilting the skillet the assure complete coverage.
  3. Dip 2 slices brioche in the egg mixture, moistening both sides.
  4. Cook brioche in the hot skillet until golden brown, 2 to 4 minutes per side. Repeat process until all bread is cooked.
  5. Cut each slice of brioche into half on a bias. Springkle hazelnuts over the halves and top with maple syrup.
